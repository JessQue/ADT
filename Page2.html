President's Challenge SY: 2019-2020

First and Second Year ADT students "Signing With You" Team wins the grand winner and given a seed funding of P15,000. 

 The President's Challenge is the ideas that lead to innovation and involves research-based and solution-focused. It happens in every year and the place was held at the Henry Sy, Sr. Innovation Center (HSSIC) in Miriam College.

"Signing With You" Team - Maria Cristina Collado, Rose Mesterio and Jessica Quejano from First Year ADT and Ros Emmanuel Ibbarientos and Isaias Cruz from Second Year ADT participated the President's Challenge for this school year 2019-2020. Signing With You is an app, a tutorial sign language that provide better communication between deaf and hearing in the community and build a bridge to understand the deaf community.
